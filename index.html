Projects

The BoombaX was a conceptual drone that would be used for mine detection in war zones. As a group, we wanted to create something that would improve the quality of life of those who have to live in these living conditions. With this concept, we also wanted to test the AI capabilities of the Arduino. For this project I realized the AI behind the movement decisions and the design of the drone.

The VDS Projects was a research project for the municipality of Tilburg. Our objective was to make a system that was able to detect whether the bat boxes, which could be found all around the city, were in use, in addition the contractor also wanted a way to measure temperature and humidity in these boxes. I had overseen the back-end side of the application and the LoRa communication for this project. Our contractor was so satisfied with the result we presented him that he wanted to take things a step further. As a result, we will work to refine and scale up the product with him, outside of school.

The WESP project was a solo research project. For this project, I wanted to create something that could replace the mouse and keyboard of a computer. Using a combination of gyroscopes and accelerometer, the WESP pointer is able to interpret movements that the user makes with their fingers. These movements are then converted to input for the computer. For the first prototype only one finger was used to control the mouse and type certain phrases. The next steps will be the usage of all fingers, which in turn could open up the possibility of using ASL for typing.

Snipe the Undead was a groupsproject that was mostly meant for fun. For this project, we wanted to bring the arcade experience to the homes of the users. Most of us grew up loving to go to the arcade, put as time passed when arcades were closed. This was our way of bringing it back. For this project, you had the game and a physical weapon which was used to aim, shoot and reload in the game. For this project, I realized the physical weapon and the Zombie AI.

Atomica was a conceptual serious game (I designed and created) meant to teach chemistry formulas in a fun way. The inspiration for this game comes from my history with chemistry. In my third year of High School, there were several chemistry formulas which we had to learn by hart. The formulas were usually simple, is a Multiplayer game in which you have to collect atoms to complete objectives in order to win.
